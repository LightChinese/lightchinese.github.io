    Meaning

    1

    kàn lái nǐ yǐ jīng fā xiàn le zhè kē tiān zhì shén míng shèng táo shù

    看来你已经发现了这棵天智神明圣桃树

    I see you have found the Sacred Peach Tree of Heavenly Wisdom.

    2

    jiù shì zhè kē shù ? bú hǎo yì sī wǒ hái yǐ wéi shì píng cháng de táo shù

    就是这棵树? 不好意思 我还以为是平常的桃树

    Is that what this is? Sorry. I thought it was just a regular peach tree.

    3

    zhè wǒ lǐ jiě nǐ xīn qíng fán zào suǒ yǐ nǐ jiù chī

    这我理解 你心情烦躁 所以你就吃

    I understand. You eat when you are upset.

    4

    xīn fán ？wǒ méi xīn fán nǐ zěn me jiào dé wǒ xīn fán ？

    心烦？我没心烦 你怎么觉得我心烦？

    Upset? I’m not upset. What makes you think I’m upset.

    5

    nǐ dào dǐ xīn lǐ zài fán xiē shí me ？

    你到底心里在烦些什么？

    So why are you so upset?

    6

    āi wǒ jīn tiān de biǎo xiàn yǒu kě néng shì lì shǐ shàng zuì làn gōng fū shǐ shàng zuì làn

    哎 我今天的表现有可能是历史上最烂 功夫史上最烂

    I probably sucked more today than anyone in the history of kung fu.

    7

    zuì làn shǐ shàng zuì làn

    最烂史上最烂

    In the history of China. In the history of sucking!

    8

    yǒu kě néng

    有可能

    Probably.

    9

    hái yǒu nà wǔ gè nǐ zhēn gāi kàn kàn tā men nà yàng tā men hèn sǐ wǒ le

    还有那五个 你真该看看他们那样 他们恨死我了

    And the Five! Man, you should’ve seen them! They totally hate me.

    10

    hèn sǐ le

    恨死了

    Totally.

    11

    shī fù zěn me cái néng bǎ wǒ xùn liàn chéng shén lóng dà xiá ne

    师父怎么才能把我训练成神龙大侠呢

    How is Shifu ever going to turn me into the Dragon Warrior?

    12

    wǒ bú shì nà wǔ gè

    我不是那五个

    I mean, I’m not like the Five.

    13

    wǒ méi yǒu lì zhǎo méi yǒu chì bǎng

    我没有利爪 没有翅膀

    I’ve got no claws, no wings, no venom.

    14

    méi yǒu dú yè lián táng láng dōu yǒu nà yàng de dōng xī

    没有毒液 连螳螂都有那样的东西

    Even Mantis has those… thingies.

    15

    āi wǒ hái shì fàng qì ba huí jiā zuò wǒ de miàn tiáo

    哎 我还是放弃吧 回家做我的面条

    Maybe I should just quit and go back to making noodles.

    16

    fàng qì ? bú fàng qì ?

    放弃? 不放弃?

    Quit, don’t quit.

    17

    zuò miàn tiáo ? bú zuò miàn tiáo ?

    做面条? 不做面条?

    Noodles, don’t noodles.

    18

    nǐ huàn dé huàn shī tài zài yì cóng qián yòu tài dān xīn jiāng lái

    你患得患失 太在意从前 又太担心将来

    You are too concerned with what was and what will be.

    19

    yǒu jù huà shuō dé hǎo

    有句话说得好

    There’s a saying:

    20

    zuó tiān shì duàn lì shǐ

    昨天是段历史

    Yesterday is history,

    21

    míng tiān shì gè mí tuán

    明天是个谜团

    tomorrow is a mystery,

    22

    ér jīn tiān shì tiān cì de lǐ wù

    而今天是天赐的礼物

    but today is a gift.

    23

    xiàng zhēn xī lǐ wù nà yàng zhēn xī jīn tiān

    像珍惜礼物那样珍惜今天

    That is why it is called the present.
