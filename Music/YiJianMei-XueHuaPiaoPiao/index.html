    真情像草原广阔

    zhēn qíng xiàng cǎo yuán guǎng kuò

    True love like the grassland so wide

    HSK Chinese Pinyin English
    2 真 zhēn real
    3 草 cǎo grass
    3 像 xiàng image
    6 广阔 guǎngkuò wide

    层层风雨不能阻隔

    céng céng fēng yǔ bú néng zǔ gé

    Unaffected by layers of wind and rain

    HSK Chinese Pinyin English
    1 不 bù no
    1 能 néng can, be able to
    3 层 céng layer

    总有云开 日出时候

    zǒng yǒu yún kāi rì chū shí hòu

    There will be a time when the clouds open and sun shines

    HSK Chinese Pinyin English
    1 开 kāi open
    1 日 rì day
    1 时候 shí hou time
    1 有 yǒu have
    2 出 chū come
    3 云 yún cloud

    万丈阳光照耀你我

    wàn zhàng yáng guāng zhào yaò nǐ wǒ

    A thousand sunbeams fall onto us

    HSK Chinese Pinyin English
    1 你 nǐ you
    1 我 wǒ I, me
    3 万 wàn ten thousand
    4 光 guāng light
    4 阳光 yángguāng sunshine
    6 照耀 zhàoyào Shine

    真情像梅花开过
